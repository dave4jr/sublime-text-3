What We Do

Mission

Bond 6 Fitness is New York City's premiere private personal training company. Founded by head trainer Blair Small, Bond 6 Fitness has one mission: transform your body and health while reaching optimum levels of fitness. Our emphasis is on ideal body composition through strength and endurance training. Bond 6 customizes training to each client's specific goals, making sure to push and challenge you just the right amount to see maximum results.

Guidance

We believe strongly in developing healthy lifestyles with our clients, through inspiration and educated guidance. It is our job to get you eating right and doing the necessary work outside of sessions to reach and sustain your goals. Training with Bond 6 is the complete fitness and health experience.

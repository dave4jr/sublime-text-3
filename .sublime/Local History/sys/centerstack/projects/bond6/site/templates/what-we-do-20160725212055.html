Our Mission

Bond 6 Fitness is New York City's premiere private personal training company. Founded by head trainer Blair Small, Bond 6 Fitness has one mission: transform your body and health while reaching optimum levels of fitness. Our emphasis is on ideal body composition through strength and endurance training. Bond 6 customizes training to each client's specific goals, making

sure to push and challenge you just the right amount to see maximum results. We believe strongly in developing healthy lifestyles with our clients, through inspiration and educated guidance. It is our job to get you eating right and doing the necessary work outside of sessions to reach and sustain your goals. Training with Bond 6 is the complete fitness and health experience.

About Blair

Blair has been training clients in New York City for over 7 years, with more than 10,000 sessions under his belt. He cares deeply about helping others reach their fitness goals, and is dedicated to teaching a lifestyle of health.

Blair is constantly expanding his knowledge and education, through coursework, new certifications, and practical experiences in the fitness industry. "We want clients to experience how good it feels to be strong, to be fit, to eat well, and to be happy about how you look. We are 100% committed to leading you there."

What We Do

STRENGTH TRAINING

Traditional and contemporary modes of strength training using several forms of resistance.

CARDIOVASCULAR TRAINING

Aerobic and anaerobic exercise for improving endurance and speed.

HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training)

A mix of high and low intensity interval exercises for a complete metabolic and fat-burning workout.

FLEXIBILITY AND MOBILITY TRAINING

Corrective exercise to improve flexibility, mobility, and muscular balance.

FUNCTIONAL TRAINING

Training for everyday activities to improve joint stability, mobility, and motor patterns.

NUTRITIONAL GUIDANCE

Nutritional advice, accountability, and motivation supported by our years of practical experience and knowledge.

Pre/post natal

From maintaining a healthy body weight, to preparing your body for the physical challenges of labor and delivery, exercise is highly recommended for a healthy pregnancy. Although the guidelines for prenatal and postpartum women are not that much different than the non-pregnant adult, there are some important recommendations and contraindications to adhere to.

Working with a professionally trained prenatal and postpartum trainer is the safest and most effective way to exercise during this period. Some of the benefits of exercise for prenatal postnatal clients are more energy during pregnancy, reduced stress and anxiety, quicker recovery from birth, fewer complications in pregnancy, shorter labor time and increased self esteem!

At Bond 6 Fitness, we are certified in pre/post natal fitness by the American Fitness Professionals & Associates Organization (AFPA).

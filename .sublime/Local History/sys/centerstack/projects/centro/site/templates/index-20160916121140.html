What We Do

entro’s founder Darrel Hansen started the small business back in 1971 when Bend’s population sign read 11,800 and the IBM Selectric typewriter was the pinnacle of office automation. For nearly 50 years Centro’s experienced professionals have brought high-tech resources and high-touch customer service to local, regional and national organizations.

Today we offer an immense assortment of printed business forms, promotional products, and branded apparel as well as services personalized to a client’s unique needs. The key to our success is insuring YOURS – we are a “relationship company” with a team devoted to exceeding client expectations by treating every Centro experience as an exceptional one!

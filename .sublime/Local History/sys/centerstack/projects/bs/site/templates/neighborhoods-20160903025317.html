Bend, Oregon

Neighborhood Guide

Naturally when someone decides to relocate to Bend, OR they want to know which neighborhoods are the best or most desirable. To help you find your perfect Bend, Oregon neighborhood, we’ve created 13 different neighborhood guides.

Bend is loosely divided into 4 quadrants – NW, SW, NE, and SE. When talking about Bend, you’ll often hear people refer to the west side or the east side with Hwy 97 being the dividing line. The west side of Bend (particularly NW Bend) is trendier and more desirable to most people because of its proximity to downtown, shops and restaurants along Newport Ave. and Galveston Ave.,

the Deschutes River, and hiking and biking trails. The north and south dividing line is less concrete. Many locals would argue that Greenwood Ave./Hwy 20 separates north from south. If we use the postal service criteria and zip codes, however, the north/south boundary is between the northern edge of the Old Mill District and the southern edge of Downtown Bend, around Simpson Ave. and Colorado Ave. on the west side and Bear Creek Rd on the east side.

About Molly

After working for ten years in Seattle’s real estate sector for one of the world’s largest commercial real estate companies, CB Richard Ellis, I shifted gears in 2002 and moved to Bend to enjoy all that Central Oregon has to offer from running the miles of trails to watching the sunset over the mountains. Currently I serve as the Designated Principal Broker for the successful and reputable firm that I work for, Total Property Resources. Being active in this role adds depth to my knowledge of the current market conditions both locally and globally. I look forward to bringing my years of real estate experience and business acumen to help you reach your real estate goals.

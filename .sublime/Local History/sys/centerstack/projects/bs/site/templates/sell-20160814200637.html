What Sets Us Apart?

Our Marketing Plan

According to the National Association of Realtors, 90% of homebuyers use the internet to search for homes. As such, it is vitally important to focus our marketing efforts online.

Equally important is exposure to the local real estate brokerage community. 89% of home buyers use the services of the real estate agent, so ensuring that your property stays top of mind with the 1,500 local brokers is imperative. We have impeccable relationships with the most active brokers in the community and we leverage those networking capabilities. Holding broker open houses, participating in MLS tours, and being fully entrenched in our local real estate community is key to the success of this type of marketing.
